What is Robert Gossett's Net Worth?

Robert Gossett is an American actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Robert Gossett was born in The Bronx, New York in March 1954. He is the cousin of actor Louis Gossett, Jr. Robert Gossett has more than 75 acting credits to his name and starred as Lt. Hudson on the television series Silk Stalkings from 1992 to 1993. From 2001 to 2004 he starred as Woody Stumper on the TV series Passions.

Gossett starred as Russell Taylor on the series The Closer from 2005 to 2012. From 2012 to 2016 he starred as Russell Taylor on the television series Major Crimes. Robert Gossett starred as Charles Ryder on the TV series The Oath in 2018. He has also appeared in several films including Over the Brooklyn Road, Batman Returns, White Man's Burden, The Maker, Arlington Road, The Inner Circle, Flying By, and more. He has been nominated for five Screen Actors Guild Awards for The Closer.
