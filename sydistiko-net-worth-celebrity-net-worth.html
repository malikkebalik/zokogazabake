What is Sydistiko's Net Worth?

Sydistiko is an American professional wrestler who has a net worth of $1 million. Paul Michael London (known also by his ring name Sydistiko) was born April 16, 1980 in Austin, Texas. He is best known for his association with the World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). London was trained at the Texas Wrestling Academy before competing for the Ring of Honor where his high-risk offensive moves made him a crowd favorite. He later competed for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling, Pro Wrestling Zero-One and independent promotions, winning multiple championships as well as the ECWA Super 8 Tournament in 2003. London made his debut in WWE in late 2003 where he was teamed with fellow cruiserweight Billy Kidman. After Kidman retired from active competition, London formed a tag team with Brian Kendrick, with whom he won the WWE Tag Team Championship.

London and Kendrick then won the WWE Tag Team Championship, becoming the longest reigning WWE Tag Team Champions since the title's creation and the World Tag Team Championship at the time. Kendrick was then drafted to the SmackDown brand in June 2008 and London was released later that year.
