What is James Valentine's Net Worth?

James Valentine is an American musician and songwriter who has a net worth of $40 million. James Valentine was born in Lincoln, Nebraska in October 1978. He is best known for being the lead guitarist of the band Maroon 5. The band formed in 1994 as Kara's Flowers. They released their debut studio album Songs About Jane in 2002 which reached #6 on the Billboard 200 and #1 in Australia, France, and the UK. Maroon 5 released the album It Won't Be Soon Before Long in 2007 and it reached #1 on the Billboard 200 chart and in the UK. Their albums Hands All Over in 2010 and Overexposed in 2012 both reached #2 on the Billboard 200 chart. Maroon 5's album V was released in 2014 and reached #1 on the Billboard 200 and in Canada. Their album Red Pill Blues in 2017 reached #2 on the Billboard 200 chart. The band's popular singles include "Harder to Breathe", "This Love", "She Will Be Loved", "Makes Me Wonder", "Moves Like Jagger" (featuring Christina Aguilera), "Payphone" (featuring Wiz Khalifa), "One More Night", "Sugar", and more.

Real Estate: In 2005, James bought a home in the Los Feliz neighborhood of Los Angeles for $2.1 million. He sold the home in November 2019 for $4.1 million to an heiress to the Getty Oil fortune. Earlier in 2019 James bought a home in the Toluca Lake area for $6.4 million from film producer Dana Brunetti.
