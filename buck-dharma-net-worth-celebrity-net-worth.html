What is Buck Dharma's Net Worth?

Buck Dharma is an American guitarist, singer, and songwriter who has a net worth of $9 million. Buck Dharma was born in Long Island, New York in November 1947. He is best known for being a member of the band Blue Oyster Cult. He was an original member of the band when they formed in 1967 and has written and sang lead vocals on many of Blue Oyster Cult's hit singles such as "(Don't Fear) The Reaper", "Burnin' for You", and "Godzilla". The band released their self-titled debut studio album in 1972 and the albums Tyranny and Mutation in 1973 and Secret Treaties in 1974.

Their album Agents of Fortune in 1976 reached #29 in the US and #26 in the UK and #28 in Canada. Blue Oyster Cult has also released the albums Mirrors in 1979, Cultosaurus Erectus in 1980, Fire of Unknown Origin in 1981, The Revolution by Night in 1983, Club Ninja in 1985, Imaginos in 1988, Heaven Forbid in 1998, and Curse of the Hidden Mirror in 2001. Their single "Burnin' for You" reached #1 on the US Rock chart. Dharma released his only solo album Flat Out in 1982.
