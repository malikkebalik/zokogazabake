Meet Evelyn Marcus Resident Evil Actress Paola Nuñez

Paola Nuñez (born April 8, 1978) is 44 years old experienced actress who has worked in 36 productions since 2001 and came into headlines many times, but on July 14, Paola amazed all viewers by making her landing in the Netflix series “Resident Evil,” in which she made her landing as Evelyn Marcus, a businesswoman and the former CEO of Umbrella Corporation.

Nuñez started her career in the late ’90s from TV shows. As time passed, Paola got work in Mexican shows and movies. Then a time came when she started getting offers from Hollywood too.

Nuñez might look familiar to you because she was also in Will Smith’s super hit movie “Bad Boys for Life” (2020) as Rita. The actress has worked in more than 3 dozen productions and is a renowned personality in the Mexican acting industry.

Birthday & Parents

In Tijuana, Baja California, Mexico, Paola Núñez Rivas (age 44) took birth on April 8, 1978. In 1980 the Núñez family shifted to Tecate, where Paola spent her life until high school graduation. Neither Paola’s father, Héctor Manuel Rivas Terán nor mother, Silvia Rivas Terán ever stopped her from acting. They always supported her in every way possible.

Her parents fully supported her even when she expressed her desire to attend acting school after schooling. In 2003 completed her training at CEFAC (Centro de Formación Actoral TV AZTECA) and devoted herself to becoming a successful actress.

Who is Paola Nuñez Boyfriend?

Yes, Nuñez is in a relationship and enjoys a strong bonded relationship with her boyfriend, Benjamin Nathan Serio. Photographic evidence proves that Paola has been dating Nathan since 2019. They met at a movie set, and their connection became more robust with time.

Benjamin is also an actor and auctioneer. He is present on Instagram as “@serioparadiso” and has more than 10k followers. The couple is globetrotters and loves to visit tropical destinations.

Acting Career

As sources say, Núñez first worked as a theatre artist, and while her acting program at CEFAC TV Azteca, she worked in several short films. From little projects, she got work in shows like “The Fall of the House of Usher” and “Like in the Movies,” which is how her career took off.

From 2001 to 2005 Paola worked in tv serials like “Súbete a mi moto,” “Tan infinito como el desierto,” “La vida es una canción,” “Lo que callamos las mujeres” and “Amor en custodia.” In 2005, she got picked for her first film “Ver, oir y caller,” a Mauricio Ochmann starer project that was directed by highly skilled director Albert Bravo.

Her performance as Maria Garcia in Brian McGreevy’s 2017 series “The Son” was unmatchable. Currently, she is a successful artist, and besides acting, she is also enjoying a fruitful modeling career.

Nuñez loves to work for society and is an open advocate of equality laws. She also financially supports organizations such as Animal Heroes, Greenpeace, and Save the Children.
