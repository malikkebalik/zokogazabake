What is Jerry Speyer's Net Worth?

Jerry Speyer is an American real estate tycoon who has a net worth of $4 billion. Born Jerry I. Speyer on June 23, 1940 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, he acquired his BA degree from Columbia College and in 1964, he managed to complete his MBA degree from Columbia Business School. Upon graduation, he joined Madison Square Garden as Assistant to the Vice President.
