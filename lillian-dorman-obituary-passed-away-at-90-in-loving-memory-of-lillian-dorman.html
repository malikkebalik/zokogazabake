A Life of Strength and Kindness

Lillian Dorman, a resident of Eagleville, Pennsylvania, and born in Norristown, Pennsylvania, peacefully passed away on December 27, 2023, at the age of 90. Throughout her life, Lillian was a pillar of strength and kindness, leaving an indelible mark on those who had the privilege of knowing her. Her unwavering love and dedication to her family and friends will forever be cherished.

A Legacy of Love

Lillian Dorman was preceded in death by her beloved husband, John, and her parents, Harry Tornetta and Anna Tornetta (Bruno). She leaves behind a loving family who will carry on her legacy. Her children, Donald Dorman Jr. and Kathleen (Jack Hannan), along with her husband, Richard Lafiata, and her grandchildren Christopher, Phillip, Stephanie, Michael, Richard, and Brandon, and great-grandchildren Blake, Reese, and Daniel, will forever cherish the memories they shared with Lillian. Together, they will continue to honor her spirit and the values she instilled in them.

Gathering to Pay Respects: Visitation and Prayer Service

Join us as we gather to pay our respects and honor the life of Lillian Dorman through a visitation and prayer service. Details of Visitation

The visitation for Lillian Dorman will take place on Wednesday, January 10th, 2024, from 11:30 AM to 12:30 PM at the Volpe Funeral Home and Cremation Services. The funeral home is located at 707 W Germantown Pike, Eagleville, Pennsylvania 19403. This gathering allows individuals to pay their respects, share stories, and offer condolences to the grieving family.

A Time for Reflection and Unity: Prayer Service
