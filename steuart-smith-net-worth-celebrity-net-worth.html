What is Steuart Smith's Net Worth?

Steuart Smith is an American musician, singer, writer, and producer who has a net worth of $10 million. Steuart Smith was born in Baltimore, Maryland in June 1952. He is best known for being the lead guitarist for the band the Eagles starting in 2001. Smith is considered a touring member of the band although he was featured on their 2007 album Long Road Out of Eden and plays guitars and mandolin and provides backing vocals. That album reached #1 in the US, Australia, Canada, the Netherlands, Norway, New Zealand, and the UK.

He also co-wrote many songs on the album. Smith joined the Eagles after Don Felder was fired. He is the band's co-lead guitar player along with Joe Walsh and harmonizes with him on the solo in "Hotel California". He was also a member of Don Henley's solo touring band and has done session work with artists including Rodney Crowell and Shawn Colvin.
