Di4ries Season 2 Release Date: When Will It Be Available On Netflix?

Season 1 of Di4ries premiered on Netflix in May of last year in Italy and other German-speaking nations, while the rest of the globe had to wait until July 26, 2022, to watch it. Alessandro Celli is in charge of directing the series. Di4ries became popular amongst people of all ages, not only the young.

There are fifteen episodes in the first season of this Italian teen drama. One of the main characters was the episode’s narrator and gave us an account of what had happened to them up to that point. This is a series about the students in Room 2D and their experiences with first loves, coming out, and overcoming adversity.

It follows the students of Marina Piccola as they cope with the sudden closure of their school owing to a lack of financing and enrollment. Many pupils show signs of distress once this devastating news is revealed.
