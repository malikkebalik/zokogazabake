Ron Vlaar net worth and salary: Ron Vlaar is a Dutch professional soccer player who has a net worth of $15 million. Ron Vlaar was born in Hensbroek, Netherlands in February 1985. He is a center back who played for Apollo '68 from 1991 to 1995, SVW '27 from 1995 to 1996, and AZ from 1996 to 2004 during his youth career. Vlaar began his senior career with AZ from 2004 to 2006. He played for Feyenoord from 2006 to 2012 where he scored 8 goals in 132 appearances. Vlaar joined Aston Villa in 2012.

He has been named one of the best defenders at the 2014 FIFA World Cup when the Netherlands finished in 3rd place. He also won the UEFA U21 Championship in 2006 and 2007.
