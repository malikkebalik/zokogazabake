What is Merlin Miller's Net Worth?

Merlin Miller is an American reality television personality and Amish leader who has a net worth of $150 thousand dollars. Merlin Miller is the leader of the Ohio Amish Community. He is featured on the hit show, "Amish Mafia", on the Discovery Channel, which follows the lives of a group of Amish men who act as protection for the Amish community in and around Lancaster, Pennsylvania. The program began airing in December of 2012 and quickly built an audience.

Merlin Miller is known for being quite eccentric and is the rival of Lancaster, Pennsylvania Amish leader Lebanon Levi. Lebanon Levi had Merlin removed from his position as head of the Ohio Amish, but he has since been reinstated. Merlin supposedly feels that Lebanon Levi is a tyrant and wants him removed. He is often shown plotting ways to unseat Lebanon Levi, with his fellow Amish enforcer, Wayne.
