What is Tom Rhodes' Net Worth?

Tom Rhodes is an American comedian, actor, television personality, and travel writer who has a net worth of $1 million. Tom Rhodes was born in Washington, D.C. in January 1967. He was the first comedian to be a spokesperson for the Comedy Central network. Rhodes is an author for the Destinations section of The Huffington Post and hosts the podcast Tom Rhodes Radio.

He has released the albums Hot Sweet Ass in 2005, Live in Paris in 2006, and Colossus of me in 2012. From 1996 to 1997 he starred as himself on the television series Mr. Rhodes. He has also appeared on episodes of the TV series The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, The Rosie O'Donnell Show, The Daily Show, Comedy Central Presents, The Joe Rogan Experience, Comedy Underground with Dave Attell, and more. Tom Rhodes has appeared in comedy clubs all across the U.S. and in international locations including Tokyo, Hong Kong, Bangkok, Singapore, Bali, London, Munich, Berlin, Vancouver, Sydney, Toronto, and more.
